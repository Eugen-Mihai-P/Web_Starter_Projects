Brief timeline of Monty's work

  • 2002: Monty begins doing independent fan videos
  • 2007: Using assets from "Super Smash Brothers" and "Halo" he creates Haloid, which later becomes popular. Later that year he begins work on his most well-known independent project, "Dead Fantasy".
  • 2008: He is hired by Namco Bandai Games as a combat designer and animator fot "Afro Samurai"
  • 2009: Monty meets Rooster Teeth co-founder Burnie Burns at a panel during San Diego Comic-Con International and the two discuss the possibility of him working for the company
  • 2010: During PAX East it's announced that Monty is working as an animator for the "Red vs Blue" series.
  • 2012: The first trailer of his own series, "RWBY" premieres.
